Research

Robustness under distribution shift

When a model trained on one distribution meets another at test time, accuracy can collapse silently. I study why this happens and design training objectives that degrade gracefully instead — useful anywhere a model outlives the data it was trained on.

arXiv/PDF/NeurIPS 2025

Efficient inference for large models

Big models are slow and expensive to serve. I study how far you can prune, quantize, or restructure them before quality breaks — and what that reveals about which parameters actually carry the work.
